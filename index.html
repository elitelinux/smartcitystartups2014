Smart

Startups - 2014

The first gathering to explore how we can help startups to solve our biggest urban challenges in areas like sustainable buildings, mobility, governance and service delivery.


100 attendees will include leading startups, investors, city representatives, foundations, researchers and experts from more than 10 cities in North America, the EU and Latin America.

City leadership and investors have critical roles to play in nurturing startups working on urban challenges. Cities can provide support via policies, procurement and partnerships. Investors can provide the funding, feedback and networks necessary to help startups succeed.

Over two days participants will share how they see the key challenges, what is working and what else we might do. Representatives from startups, investors and cities will have a chance to share their particular perspectives. Promising cases and strategies will be documented and shared publicly and participants will be invited to continue working together online.

The event will take place in Miami on April 24/25.
On the evening of April 24, an open house will be held to showcase leading startup solutions.

The event is made possible by Urban.Us, Knight Foundation, The LAB and American Airlines. To enable interactive formats and ensure that attendees have an opportunity to meet one another, the event is limited to 100 participants.
